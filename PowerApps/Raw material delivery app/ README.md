##  Process Workflow
<img width="960" height="64" alt="raw_material_delivery_process" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5a860970-ba01-4fa7-9864-15d3c8c4c5f3" />


**Workflow Steps:**
1. **Warehouse operator** completes delivery form in PowerApps.  
2. **Power Automate** sends a notification to Quality Control.  
3. **Quality Control** reviews documents and approves delivery.  
4. **Warehouse** proceeds with unloading and confirms completion.  
5. **Power BI** dashboard updates with delivery data & usage stats.
